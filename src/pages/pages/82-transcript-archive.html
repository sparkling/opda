Working with transcripts

  • UTF-8 plain text. No timestamps for YouTube auto-captions; speaker breaks usually appear as newlines.
  • For grep/search across the corpus from your terminal: rg "term" source/ is fastest. From the docs site, use the resource viewer's iframe + browser Ctrl-F.
  • For LLM-friendly bulk read, the transcripts at source/05-engagement/videos-youtube/transcripts/ total ~250 KB of text and fit comfortably in a single context window.
