Engagement — overview

OPDA is convening. Most of the deliverable work — alignment with regulators, scoping with members, resolving disagreements, communicating publicly — happens in a small set of recurring bodies and content channels.

DPMSG is the senior coordination forum. OPDA leads four of its six working groups. Member updates and the YouTube channel are OPDA's own communication surfaces.

How a new participant engages

  1. Read the OPDA About us and the Code of Conduct.
  2. Join an all-member update (see Meetings & decisions) — recorded, open to members.
  3. Pick a working group — see DPMSG working groups for the six current ones.
  4. Apply for the Sandbox via the published cohort process — see Introduction-to-the-Sandbox-Setup-Guide.pdf.
  5. Watch the video library for the 1.5 hour induction — see Video library.
