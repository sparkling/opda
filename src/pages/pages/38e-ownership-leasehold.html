Ownership — leasehold transfer

When the ownership being transferred is leasehold, this cluster is the assignment of the lease — its term, the people who own and manage it, ground rent, service charge, consents, and assorted legal artefacts. Eight sub-pages each scope one part of the leasehold disclosure.

Pages in this cluster

  • Term & shared ownership — lease term, shared-ownership percentage and rent schedule.
  • Contact list — freeholder, management company, agent, sinking-fund holder.
  • Service-contact roles — which contact does what.
  • Management — ownership-and-management structure and obligations.
  • Ground rent — schedule, escalation, payment terms.
  • Service charge & insurance — service-charge accounts, buildings insurance schedule.
  • Consents, restrictions, alterations, safety — landlord consents, lease restrictions, alterations, enfranchisement, Building Safety Act.
  • Disputes, general, documents, confirmation — disputes, general declarations, required documents, accuracy confirmation.
