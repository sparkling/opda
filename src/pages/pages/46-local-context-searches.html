Schema Page 46 Landing

Local context & searches

Everything outside the property boundary that affects it — what the local authority records, what the Local Land Charges register lists, and what the environmental searches return. Three sub-pages by search type.

The shape of this cluster

Each search has its own authority, its own reply schema, and its own validity window. CON29R is the local-authority enquiries (planning, highways, contaminated land). LLC1 is the statutory charges register entry list. Environmental searches stitch in flood, mining, ground stability, and contaminated land risk. CON29DW (water and drainage) is a notable absence in this schema cut — there is no dedicated path for it yet; water-related leaves live on the Utilities & energy page.

Pages in this cluster

  • Local authority (CON29R) — local-authority identity and CON29R replies; listing & conservation.
  • Local Land Charges (LLC1) — LLC1 register entries; other one-off searches.
  • Environmental — flood, mining, ground stability, contaminated land.
