Fixtures & fittings

The TA10 schedule: every item that's either included with the sale, removed, or available for separate purchase, broken out room by room. Ten sub-pages, one per area of the property.

Pages in this cluster

  • Items to include / remove — top-level inclusions and exclusions, accuracy confirmation.
  • Basic fittings — boilers, hot-water tanks, radiators, immersions, etc.
  • Kitchen — fitted units, white-goods, extractor.
  • Bathroom — fittings, sanitary-ware, showers.
  • Carpets — by room.
  • Curtains & rails — by room.
  • Light fittings — by room.
  • Fitted units — wardrobes, shelving, cupboards.
  • Outdoor — sheds, plants, garden furniture.
  • TV, telephone, fuel, other items — TV/telephone install, stock of fuel, other.
