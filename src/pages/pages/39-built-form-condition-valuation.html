Built form, condition & valuation

The physical-building cluster — how the property is built, what condition it's in, what's fixed to it, what surveyors say about it, and what it's worth. Five sub-pages by object sub-cluster.

The shape of this cluster

Built form is the structural description; condition is what's gone wrong or been altered; fixtures & fittings is the schedule of what travels with the sale; surveys are the professional assessments; valuation is the agreed money. The shared lifecycle is inspection → report → settlement.

Pages in this cluster

  • Built form — construction, year, type, parking, smart-home systems, summary & media.
  • Condition — alterations, electrical works, defects, declared condition.
  • Fixtures & fittings — full TA10 schedule by room: items to include / remove, kitchen, bathroom, carpets, curtains, lights, outdoor, services.
  • Surveys — RICS Home Survey structure: grounds, inside (roof / floors / finishes), outside (chimneys / envelope / extras), services, legal, valuation block, advice & risks.
  • Valuation — valuations and price information.
