Legal estate & title

The legal layer of the property pack — who owns it, on what tenure, what the title register records, and what binds the estate. This is the largest cluster in the PDTF schema; it splits into seven sub-pages by object sub-cluster.

The shape of this cluster

Tenure governs the conveyance. Title — registered with HMLR — carries the seal of state behind every ownership claim and every charge or restriction over the estate. Ownership describes how the seller holds the estate and what they transfer at completion (freehold, leasehold, managed). Boundaries and rights complete the legal envelope.

Pages in this cluster

  • Tenure — freehold/leasehold/commonhold marketing tenure and the legal-owner roster.
  • Title — Official Copy summary — parsed OC summary: proprietorship, lease, charges, restrictions, notices.
  • Title — Official Copy full register — raw register data and additional title documents.
  • Ownership — freehold transfer — what's transferred when the estate is freehold.
  • Ownership — leasehold transfer — lease term, ground rent, service charge, management, consents.
  • Ownership — managed — managed freehold / commonhold contacts, finances, disputes.
  • Boundaries & rights — legal boundaries and informal arrangements over the estate.
