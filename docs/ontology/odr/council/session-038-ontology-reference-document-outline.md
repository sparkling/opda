## Context + the pre-flight finding

The operator asked the council to *produce* the outline for a standalone single-page HTML document covering "every aspect" of the ontology, and to *validate* it sound and complete. The pre-flight surfaced one decisive fact that reframed the whole proposition: **the ontology is generator-emitted and anti-drift by doctrine.** Every TTL opens `# Generated by opda-gen … DO NOT HAND-EDIT`; ODR-0004 §6a mandates deterministic emission under a **byte-identity CI gate** (`make verify-ontology`); every term carries `dct:source`; releases carry `owl:versionIRI`. A hand-authored monolith documenting ~58 classes, 256 properties, ~50 SKOS schemes / ~318 concepts, ~58 SHACL shapes, 31 profiles and 17 exemplars would, by construction, possess none of those properties and would drift from the corpus on the first commit, with no gate to catch it.

This made the DA's standing methodology (Peroni) not a contrarian foil but the **central engineering question**: should this be hand-authored at all, or generated? The panel's convergence resolved it into a hybrid.

**Grounded pre-flight verifications (Queen):**
- The **non-conformant BASPI5 exemplar's expected report reads `sh:conforms "true"`** (`exemplars/baspi5-transaction-nonconformant-expected-report.ttl`) — Knublauch's claim is confirmed. The intended violation surfaces **only under SHACL-AF** (`sh:xone` passes vacuously under SHACL-Core). This is a load-bearing soundness caveat the document must carry.
- **Namespace drift (grounded, inverted from the DA's framing):** the *emitted* `foundation.ttl` correctly uses `https://opda.org.uk/pdtf/` (post-migration, ADR-0006 as-built), but **ODR-0004's prose still cites `https://w3id.org/opda/#` as the ratified base** (§Rules.1, §Consequences, the diagram). The drift is real — it is ODR-0004 that is stale, not the artefact. This *strengthens* the DA's thesis (hand-maintained prose drifts) and is logged as a known-issue + a separate cleanup.
- SKOS counts differ by reader (Cagle 47/308; whole-dir scan 50/318) — the document MUST emit **live generator counts**, never hardcode them.

## Questions + verdicts

- **Q1 — Section spine & order.** "The outline's section set and ordering is correct." **REVISE. AFFIRM 2 (Knublauch, Moreau) / REVISE 4 (Peroni, Guizzardi, Cagle, Allemang) / REJECT 0.** Carries with reordering: **definition before constraint** (classes/properties/vocabularies precede shapes/profiles — shapes reference terms) and the **conceptual/UFO frame leads** the whole document.
- **Q2 — Completeness (every aspect).** "The outline covers every aspect of the ontology." **REVISE. AFFIRM 1 (Guizzardi) / REVISE 4 (Knublauch, Cagle, Moreau, Allemang) / REJECT 1 (Peroni).** Completeness redefined as **"every UFO stereotype, every IC, every SKOS scheme, every SHACL shape, every profile, every governance layer has a documented home — plus the honesty items"** (gap register, known defects, thin-profile status, retired-programme banner). "Every aspect" as an eager hand-maintained enumeration is rejected (Peroni); completeness is a *category* gate, not a hand-typed term dump.
- **Q3 — Soundness (no section misrepresents the model).** "Each section's framing is semantically correct." **REVISE with must-fix conditions. AFFIRM 1 (Cagle) / REVISE 3 (Guizzardi, Moreau, Allemang) / REJECT 2 (Peroni, Knublauch).** The two REJECTs are conditional and lift once the must-fixes land: (a) never render inferred triples as asserted (asserted-vs-entailed callout per module, OWL-RL ODR-0025/0026); (b) never present Roles/RoleMixins in a subclass tree (ODR-0027 §R6); (c) every shape states the **graph it targets** + the **SHACL-AF dependency**; (d) the evidence model reflects ODR-0027 §R6 (RoleMixin + `evidenceType` facet, not the retired subclass tree); (e) soundness is **mechanically tied to source** (generated body), not hand-asserted once.
- **Q4 — Single-page monolith vs generated per-term; mandatory navigation.** "A single hand-authored HTML page is the right artefact." **REVISE. AFFIRM 0 / REVISE 5 (Guizzardi, Knublauch, Cagle, Moreau, Allemang) / REJECT 1 (Peroni).** Unanimous on the **hybrid**: single-page *delivery surface* = hand-authored shell + generated term-reference body, one page, emitted by `opda-gen`, guarded by a doc-drift CI gate. Mandatory navigation (sticky TOC; per-term anchors `id` = IRI local name, CI-enforced; collapsible default-collapsed modules; client-side search; UFO-category index). Constraints rendered **resolved**, never as raw blank nodes.
- **Q5 — Machine / consumer content.** "The outline includes the machine/consumer affordances." **AFFIRM. AFFIRM 4 (Guizzardi, Knublauch, Cagle, Allemang) / REVISE 2 (Peroni, Moreau) / REJECT 0.** Required: prefix/namespace map; runnable SPARQL examples; dereferenceability / content-negotiation statement (opda-direct serving); a **SHACL-validation recipe** (load class + shapes + profile graphs; **pyshacl `advanced=True` REQUIRED**; read `sh:ValidationReport` fields); per-module + bundled TTL downloads; the three derived profiles (`opda-validation`/`opda-ui`/`opda-inference`). REVISEs: this content must itself be generated scaffolding citing live URIs, not hand-paraphrased (else it re-commits the ODR-0018 Option-D `rdfs:comment`-only failure).
- **Q6 — Provenance / governance depth + known-issues.** "Provenance, governance and known-issues are represented at the right depth." **AFFIRM. AFFIRM 4 (Guizzardi, Knublauch, Cagle, Allemang) / REVISE 2 (Peroni, Moreau) / REJECT 0.** Required: a claims/evidence/provenance section (PROV-O backbone + three truth-makers: derivation / signature / assurance); a DPV governance/PII section (referenced-not-imported, `isPIIBearing`, lawful-basis, purpose scheme); term-provenance (`dct:source` → schema-leaf-path); decision-provenance (ODR/ADR/council lineage, **programme RETIRED 2026-06-14**, ODR-0016 waived); **held-as-live dissents with re-open triggers**; and a **dated, machine-cross-referenced Known-Issues register**.

## Dialectic

**The drift argument is the spine of the session (Peroni, DA → adopted).** Peroni's attack — "a hand-built monolith documenting *every aspect* is an anti-pattern grafted onto a byte-identity-gated, `DO NOT HAND-EDIT` corpus; it is wrong on the first commit; 'every aspect' is an un-finishable wall with no per-term dereferenceable anchor" — was not refuted by any voice. It was **absorbed**. Every panellist independently partitioned the artefact the same way: the **per-term reference layer must be generated from the TTL**, and the **conceptual/rationale/governance layer must be hand-authored** because a generator cannot emit *why* `opda:Seller` is an anti-rigid RoleMixin, *why* UPRN is a contingent Quality and never the identity criterion, or *why* DPV is referenced-not-imported. The synthesis — **generated content, hand-authored frame, one navigable page, one CI gate** — is the unanimous Q4 outcome and the DA's own re-open trigger. The DA therefore **WITHDREW** on Q2/Q3/Q4: the council mandates exactly the two conditions he set (per-term reference is generated, not hand-authored; a doc-drift CI gate fails the build on divergence).

**Soundness is the load-bearing constraint (Guizzardi + Knublauch — the two REJECTs that became must-fixes).** Guizzardi's gravest red-flag: documenting Roles in an `rdfs:subClassOf` tree would re-introduce the exact anti-pattern ODR-0027 §R6 retired — the document must render Roles/RoleMixins by founding-Relator, never in the class hierarchy. His second: never list entailed triples beside asserted ones (OWL-RL materialises triples; an asserted-vs-entailed callout per module is mandatory). Knublauch's: a shape table without a **target column** is decorative, not normative; and the document must carry the **SHACL-AF caveat** because the committed non-conformant exemplar *conforms under SHACL-Core* — a doc claiming "bad data fails" without `advanced=True` is provably wrong against the corpus. Both REJECTs are conditional; they lift to the REVISE consensus once the must-fixes are in the outline (they are — §§3, 6, 9, 15 below).

**Completeness is a category gate, not a term dump (Allemang, Queen + Moreau).** The panel rejected "every aspect = every term, hand-typed." Completeness is checked against *kinds of content*: every UFO stereotype, every IC, every scheme, every shape, every profile, every governance layer has a home — and crucially the **honesty items** (Moreau): the document must not claim "sound + complete" while hiding the EPC-certificate inference defect (ODR-0028 R3, OPEN), the 1095 GAPped profile leaves (session-034), the 16 thin profiles, or the retired-programme status. A completeness claim that conceals deferred work or known defects is a provenance lie about the ontology's own lifecycle.

**Navigability is the price of one page (Cagle).** With ~600 addressable terms + 31 profiles + 300+ concepts, eager rendering produces a multi-MB DOM that janks and defeats Ctrl-F; raw SHACL blank nodes (`_:b01568…`) are unreadable. The single page survives "every aspect" only with: collapsible default-collapsed `<details>` per scheme/profile/class, lazy rendering, a client-side search over a flat manifest, anchors equal to IRI local names (CI-enforced so deep links don't rot), and constraints rendered **resolved** ("builtForm ∈ {Detached, Semi-detached, …}, minCount 1") never as blank-node chains.

## The agreed outline (the deliverable)

A single HTML page emitted by `opda-gen`. **[HAND]** = hand-authored narrative (editorial judgement a generator cannot produce). **[GEN]** = generated from the committed TTL/annotations at build time; never hand-edited; covered by the doc-drift CI gate.

0. **Front matter [HAND].** Title; `owl:versionInfo`/`versionIRI`; **dated status banner** (programme RETIRED 2026-06-14, historical anchor + fresh-ODR continuation); build date; generator version + source commit; licence (CC0); **regeneration-provenance banner** declaring the [HAND]/[GEN] split.
1. **Navigation chrome [GEN].** Sticky TOC; per-term anchors (`id` = IRI local name, CI-enforced); collapsible per-module sections (default-collapsed for the 300+ concepts / 31 profiles); client-side search/filter; UFO-category index.
2. **Prefix / namespace map [GEN].** `opda:`, `opda-v:`, `sh:`, `dash:`, `dct:`, `prov:`, `skos:`, `dpv:`/`dpv-pd:` + the ADR-0006 kind-split (`/pdtf/` terms · `/pdtf/scheme/` SKOS · `/pdtf/shape/` shapes · `/pdtf/graph/` · `/pdtf/harness/`) and the collision-avoidance rationale (`opda:role` property vs `opda-v:role` scheme).
3. **Foundational stance & architecture [HAND].** Purpose; **UFO meta-category legend** mapping every class to its stereotype (rigid Substance Kind / Role / RoleMixin / Relator / Mode / Quality / value-structure) with OntoClean meta-properties; three-graph separation (classes ⊥ shapes ⊥ annotations); the six bounded-context partition; **OWL-RL inference-regime narrative + asserted-vs-entailed discipline** (ODR-0025/0026). *Leads, before any term table.*
4. **Identity criteria & hard cases [HAND].** The implicit-Property crux (ODR-0005); the `Property`/`LegalEstate`/`RegisteredTitle` split; each IC over its 5 named hard cases; **UPRN as contingent Quality, never the IC, never `owl:sameAs`**.
5. **Classification doctrine — facets, not subclass trees [HAND].** ODR-0027: `isMemberOf` coded-SKOS default; `isA` only for genuine Kinds; Roles anti-rigid, never subclass a Kind; the facet terminology-overload caveat; the evidence re-model (§R6: `Evidence` is RoleMixin + `evidenceType` facet; subclasses retired).
6. **Class reference [GEN].** Per class: IRI · anchor · UFO stereotype · IC field (Kinds) · `rdfs:comment` · `dct:source` · governing ODR/ADR. Asserted-vs-entailed marked.
7. **Property reference [GEN].** Object + datatype properties; domain/range/cardinality; `dct:source`; governing ODR. Rendered **resolved**, never raw blank node.
8. **SKOS vocabularies [GEN].** Sortable index (**live count**, prefLabel, member count, `ufoCategory`/`scopeNote`, steward); per scheme its members with addressable concept IRI, prefLabel, definition, `dct:source`.
9. **SHACL base shapes [GEN].** **Shape-count reconciliation** (base NodeShapes vs profile shapes); per shape: **target column** (`sh:targetClass`/`targetSubjectsOf`/`targetNode`) · resolved property constraints · **severity tier** (Info/Warning/Violation = regulatory weight, ODR-0013); the JSON-Schema→SHACL mapping table (ODR-0013).
10. **Overlay profiles / forms [GEN index + HAND doctrine].** "A form IS its SHACL overlay" (ADR-0029 / S022); index all 31 with **thin-vs-bound** columns + THIN-by-design badge (session-034); the **gap register** (224 bound / 1095 GAPped); each profile's `dct:source` external instrument + `ValidationContext` + `formVersion`.
11. **Claims / Evidence / Provenance [HAND narrative + GEN tables].** PROV-O backbone (`Claim ⊑ prov:Entity`, qualified attribution); the **three truth-makers** (derivation / signature / assurance); the ~80%/five-exceptions boundary; the evidence RoleMixin model.
12. **Data governance / PII [HAND narrative + GEN tables].** DPV referenced-not-imported (`dct:references`, no `owl:imports`); `isPIIBearing` on the 6 baseline Kinds; special-category (Art.10); lawful-basis via `DPVMappingRecord`; the purpose SKOS scheme (emission-gated).
13. **Machine / consumer section [HAND recipe + GEN links].** Prefix table (xref §2); runnable SPARQL; dereferenceability / content-negotiation (opda-direct); **SHACL-validation recipe — pyshacl `advanced=True` REQUIRED**; per-module + bundled TTL downloads; the three derived profiles.
14. **Governance & decision-provenance [HAND inline rulings + linked transcripts].** Term-provenance (`dct:source` → schema-leaf-path, ODR-0004); the ODR/ADR/council lineage; **programme retirement** (ODR-0003 RETIRED, ODR-0016 waived, ODR-0028 reconciliation); **held-as-live dissents with re-open triggers** (Davis on ODR-0009; Allemang on ODR-0012).
15. **Known-issues register [HAND, DATED, machine-cross-referenced].** Each row: term anchor · governing ODR · status · date. MUST include: (i) **EPC-certificate inference cross-trip** (ODR-0028 R3, OPEN); (ii) **SHACL-Core vacuous `sh:xone`** — the non-conformant exemplar reads `conforms true` without SHACL-AF; (iii) **1095 GAPped leaves / 16 thin profiles** (by design); (iv) Category-C binning report miscount; (v) **ODR-0004 namespace prose drift** (`w3id` vs `opda.org.uk`).
16. **Full index & glossary [GEN].** Alphabetical term index + cross-reference; a "how to cite a term" note (stable fragment IDs).
17. **Regeneration footer [HAND + GEN].** How to rebuild from the TTL; the **doc-drift CI gate** that fails the build on divergence (the binding outcome of the DA's re-open trigger).

## Dispositions

- **P → REVISE.** Carries as the hybrid above. Routed to **ADR-0041** (engineering: production model, section structure, doc-drift CI gate). The ODR/ADR boundary holds — this is a schema-encoding/artefact-production decision (an ADR), not an ontology-modelling decision.
- **DA disposition — WITHDRAWN (Q2/Q3/Q4).** The council adopts Peroni's two conditions as binding outcomes: the per-term reference body is **generated**, and a **doc-drift CI gate** fails the build on divergence. **Re-open trigger (held as an implementation acceptance gate):** if the build ships a hand-authored term reference, OR omits the doc-drift CI gate, OR makes the gate green by suppression, the objection re-opens.
- **Must-fix soundness conditions (Guizzardi, Knublauch)** are binding on §§3/6/7/9/11/15 — Roles never in a subclass tree; asserted-vs-entailed marked; shape target + SHACL-AF caveat present; evidence as RoleMixin+facet; constraints resolved not blank-node.
- **Separate cleanup (Queen):** repair the ODR-0004 namespace prose drift (`w3id.org/opda/#` → `opda.org.uk/pdtf/` as-built, ADR-0006) — logged as known-issue (v) and as a standalone follow-up.
- The document carries **`status: draft`** until the CI gate is green; the operator ratifies publication.
