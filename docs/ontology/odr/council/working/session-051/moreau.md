## Opening

PROV's relations are the well-founded inter-entity edges OPDA already authors; the defect is rendering, not modelling. Minting `opda:` synonyms for PROV terms violates §R1 parsimony and orphans existing triples.

- **Q1** NameChangeEvent→Person USE-EXISTING-PROV (`prov:wasRevisionOf`); LeaseExtension/UPRNSuccession USE-EXISTING-PROV (`prov:wasDerivedFrom`); Milestone→Transaction GATED — partonomy; PROV-DM §5.3 deliberately has no part-of-activity, so a bespoke partonomic predicate is required.
- **Q2** Survey/Search/Comparable/NearbyFacility→Property: PROV has no "is-about" topic relation (`prov:specializationOf` ≠ subject-of) → aboutness edge warranted. *(disposition revised to RESIDUE-PENDING in cross-talk.)* AttachedDocument reached via its Evidence (Q4).
- **Q3** LeaseTerm VALUE-SLOT — `time:ProperInterval` is −I (PROV-DM §5.7 treats intervals as attributes of events, not entities).
- **Q4** VerificationActivity→Claim/Evidence/AttachedDocument USE-EXISTING-PROV — canonical `prov:used` + `prov:qualifiedAttribution`/`wasAssociatedWith` pattern; ODR-0009 designed exactly this, emit don't mint.
- **Q5** Transaction→LegalEstate `concerns` GATED — aboutness PROV doesn't carry, both +I, query-motivated; chain DEFERRED→ (revised: declare+gate, exemplar exists).
- **Q6** FOR — subsumption to `prov:Activity`/`prov:Entity` is the orphans' navigational backbone (*Provenance: An Introduction to PROV* ch.2).

## Cross-talk

**(1) "PROV is silent on aboutness" — defended, qualified per class, against Kendall.** Kendall conflates *provenance lineage* with *topical aboutness*. `prov:wasDerivedFrom` asserts causal derivation; `prov:wasInformedBy` links two activities. Neither says "Entity is *about* subject X."
- **Search** *is* `prov:wasDerivedFrom` a register, but the target is the register, not the Property — Property-aboutness still un-said.
- **Survey, Comparable, NearbyFacility** are NOT derived from the Property (a Survey is generated by an inspection Activity); `prov:wasDerivedFrom Property` would be a **false provenance claim**. So `opda:aboutProperty` is not a PROV duplicate; PROV-DM §5 has no topic/isAbout term.

**(2) Zero exemplars → I change Round-1 GATE to RESIDUE-PENDING.** §R1(b) needs a worked query; convener verified ZERO exemplars. Register `opda:aboutProperty` (one subject-guarded `sh:or` predicate, per Cagle); gate the instant an exemplar+query lands. Aligns me with Guarino/Davis on evidentiary discipline while preserving the warrant against Kendall's dissolve.

**(3)** Milestone→Transaction CONFIRMED bespoke partonomic predicate, now reclassified **declare+gate** (used-but-undeclared). Comparable→Valuation CONFIRMED USE-EXISTING `prov:wasInformedBy`.
