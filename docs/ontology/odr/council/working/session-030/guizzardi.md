## The pro-Kind case, at full strength (then where it stops)

I will not soften the witness. ODR-0005 §3a hard-case 4 ("Replacement") establishes something stronger than functional unity: it establishes that **a built structure can persist as the same individual when the Property's spatial-material identity is overridden** by a legal-record discontinuity, and conversely that a routine knock-down-rebuild *preserves* a Property while *replacing* its structure. The two endurants therefore have **identity criteria that demonstrably diverge** — the structure tracks a different individuation principle (constructed-fabric continuity) than the parcel (spatial-material continuity with legal override). Under UFO that divergence is the signature of a genuine **+O** (it supplies its own principle of identity): a thing that can outlive, or be outlived by, the thing it is currently part of is **not** identity-dependent on its host, and is therefore a candidate Substance Sortal in its own right — exactly as `opda:RegisteredTitle` earned Kind-hood in ODR-0005 by the temporal-extent argument (exists before the LegalEstate is vested, after it is dissolved). **Building is the same shape of argument.** The witness points, precisely, at *Building* — "a built structure persists" names a *building*, not a *room*. So on Q2 I hold the line my role assigns me: **Building's eventual IC is real, +O, and not inherited.** That is settled and I affirm it without hedging.

Now the honest part, which my own UFO commitments force. A genuine IC establishes that Building **is a Kind in principle**. It does **not**, on its own, establish that we **mint and populate the class now**. UFO is a theory of *what individuals there are and how we track them*; a Kind is the meta-category for a sortal **whose instances are individuated**. Minting a `rdfs:Class` is an ontological commitment that **these instances exist in the data and are re-identified by their IC**. Three facts decide that second question, and they all cut the same way:

1. **The witness is latent, not exercised.** session-026 (Guarino, with my concurrence) already ruled the +O IC genuine *and* deferred — because conjunct (b) of the promotion test ("a consumer query re-identifies its instances by that IC") is **unmet**. Nothing in the corpus re-identifies a Building or a Room as an individual across surveys, across Properties, or across the Replacement event. The IC exists in the *theory of the domain*; it is **unexercised in the data**.
2. **There is no individuation token in the data to carry instance identity.** `roomDimensions.rooms[]` is a bare repeating group of `{roomName, description, length, width, units}`. `roomName` is a **non-rigid label** ("Bedroom 2", "Reception") — it is a mode-of-presentation, not a principle of identity (you cannot re-identify *the same room* across two dated floorplans by its name; rooms get renamed, merged, re-measured). BASPI5 carries **no positional token**. A Kind without instance-identity-bearers in the data is a Kind we *assert* but cannot *track* — precisely the "schema with RDF syntax, not an ontology" failure ODR-0005 was created to cure, inverted: minting `opda:Room` now would re-commit that defect for the sub-part layer.
3. **The values are losslessly carried by a quality-value structure** — and we already have the precedent. This is what makes the un-individuated group a **structure**, not a thing.

The distinction I want on the record, in UFO terms: **functional unity is necessary but not sufficient for a *modelled* Kind.** A house has functional parts (rooms, walls, a roof) and a building has genuine functional unity — that is uncontested OntoUML. But OntoUML mints a Kind for a functional complex only when its **instances are individuated and tracked by an identity principle that does work**. Here the work is absent. What the data actually contains is *measurements organised by a value structure* — a length, a width, a unit, under a human-readable label — i.e. a **Quality-value record**, the same meta-category as `opda:MonetaryAmount`.

## The `opda:MonetaryAmount` precedent is exactly on point (ODR-0024 R3)

I authored the MonetaryAmount disposition and the council recorded it as Guizzardi-required, so I am consistent in applying it. MonetaryAmount is a **by-value quality-value structure**: a magnitude (`opda:amount`) + a currency (`opda:currency`), with a **by-value identity criterion** — "two nodes denote the same amount iff equal magnitude AND equal currency" — explicitly **NOT an endurant** and **NOT a Quality of any bearer**. It is reused as the `rdfs:range` across distinct per-economic-kind properties (`annualGroundRent`, `rent`, `soldPrice`…), **each bound to its own bearer** — *reuse the value type, never the bearer*.

A room dimension is the **same kind of object**: a structured value `{length, width, unit}` (optionally a `roomName` label), with a **by-value IC** (two room-dimension records are the same record iff equal length AND equal width AND equal unit AND equal name). It is a Quality-value structure of the Property, not an endurant. The repeating group `rooms[]` is therefore most faithfully modelled as an **anonymous by-value structure** ranged over the Property — `opda:hasRoomDimension → opda:RoomDimension` (a structured datum, *not* a Substance Kind), or, if the council prefers maximal minimality and there is no within-record reasoning, as **flat repeating datatype triples** on the Property. Either way: **no `opda:Room` endurant, no instance identity asserted that the data cannot support.**

## Per-question verdicts

### Q1 — `opda:Room`: class / by-value structure / flat? + UFO category + IC

**REJECT (mint a class now); AFFIRM by-value structure as the home.** `opda:Room` is **not** a modelled Kind today. The repeating group is un-individuated (no positional token; `roomName` is a non-rigid label, not an IC), so its instances cannot be re-identified — a Kind we cannot track is one we must not assert (the inverse of the ODR-0005 implicit-Property cure). **UFO meta-category: a Quality-value structure** (`opda:RoomDimension`, a structured datum carrying `length`/`width`/`unit`/`roomName`), ranged over `opda:Property` by-value, exactly mirroring `opda:MonetaryAmount` (ODR-0024 R3). **By-value IC:** two room-dimension records are identical iff equal magnitude-tuple and label — there is *no* +O endurant IC because there is no endurant. If the council wants maximal minimality over a within-record query check, flat repeating datatype properties on `opda:Property` are an acceptable weaker form; I prefer the named by-value structure because it keeps the three measurement leaves grouped as one value (the unit binds the two magnitudes) without committing to a tracked individual. Confirms session-026 and ODR-0024 R10.

### Q2 — `opda:Building`: class now? + UFO category + IC — the witness points at Building

**REVISE — affirm Kind-in-principle (+O, genuine), REJECT minting now.** I hold, at full strength, that Building's eventual IC is **real and +O**: the ODR-0005 §3a Replacement witness shows a built structure persisting through a Property-identity override (and being replaced while the Property persists), so its identity principle (constructed-fabric continuity) **demonstrably diverges** from the parcel's — the same temporal-extent argument that earned `opda:RegisteredTitle` its Kind. **UFO meta-category (eventual): Substance Kind** (a functional complex, rigid, supplies own IC; DOLCE PhysicalObject), the structural sibling of `opda:Property`. **But "is a Kind in principle" ≠ "populate the class now":** the witness is **latent** — no consumer query re-identifies a Building instance, and the only Building-level facts in the data (`numberOfFloors`, `builtForm`) are **already flat Qualia on `opda:Property`** and are answered losslessly there. Minting an unpopulated, untracked `opda:Building` buys an endurant the data never individuates. **Hold; the +O is affirmed and recorded as the firing rationale, not denied.**

### Q3 — Mereology: functional componenthood? transitivity? weak supplementation

**AFFIRM the analysis; REJECT asserting the relations now (no relata to bear them).** *In principle*, Room `componentOf` Building and Building `componentOf` Property are **functional-part (componentOf) relations**, not arbitrary parthood — functional parts in UFO are **non-transitive** (a room is a functional part of a building, a building a functional part of a property, but a room is **not** a functional component of the *property* in the same functional sense — the functional role is mediated), so the council must **not** assert a transitive `partOf`. **Weak supplementation** (every whole with a proper part has another disjoint part) is satisfiable in principle (a building has rooms *and* a roof *and* walls) but is **vacuous to assert today** because we have minted no Building/Room individuals to supplement. Operationally: until Q1/Q2 mint endurants (they do not, this session), there is **no mereology to encode** — a by-value `RoomDimension` is a *quality value of the Property*, related by `opda:hasRoomDimension` (a datum-valued property), **not** a `componentOf` between two endurants. Encode mereology only when the firing trigger mints the relata.

### Q4 — length / width / roomName: Quale-in-Region; units; roomName non-rigidity

**AFFIRM.** `length` and `width` are **Qualia projected into a one-dimensional length Region** — and a Quale is individuated *only together with its Region (unit)*, so the magnitude and its unit are **one value structure**, not two independent flat decimals. This is the decisive UFO reason to keep `{length, width, unit}` grouped (by-value `RoomDimension`) rather than scatter three sibling datatype triples: a bare `length=4.2` with the unit elsewhere is an under-specified Quale. **Units:** only `opda:UnitOfAreaScheme` exists (area); the per-room `units` enum (meters/centimetres/millimetres/feet/inches) is a **linear-measure value-space with no scheme today** — mint a small `opda:UnitOfLengthScheme` (SKOS, ODR-0011 territory) so the Quale's Region is a dereferenceable concept, *exactly* as `opda:CurrencyScheme` gives `MonetaryAmount` its currency Region; do **not** leave it an opaque string. **`roomName` is non-rigid** — a Phase/role-ish label or mode-of-presentation, **never** an identity principle (confirms ODR-0024 R10); it carries no individuation and must not be promoted to a key.

### Q5 — Realisation + minimality

**AFFIRM minimality; REVISE the realisation framing.** The minimal faithful commitment is: **mint nothing endurant** — one by-value `opda:RoomDimension` structure (or flat repeating datatype properties) ranged over `opda:Property`, plus a `opda:UnitOfLengthScheme`. The realisation/`realises` apparatus (a built STRUCTURE *realising* a Property, the latent IC of session-026) is **correct as the deferred design** but must **not** be wired now: with zero Building/Room individuals there is nothing to stand on either side of a `realises` relation, so asserting it would be decorative (the same "decorative-under-entailment" failure ODR-0008 §Q6a guards against). Preserve the zero-cost upgrade path already noted in session-026 (the dormant `PropertyTypeScheme`→subclass binding); the by-value structure **re-homes cleanly** onto a minted `opda:Room`/`opda:Building` when the re-identification trigger fires, losing nothing — precisely because a value structure makes no instance-identity claim to retract.
