- **Record:** ODR-0023 **R3** reconvened — `opda:Room` / `opda:Building` class promotion — under a directing-authority override of the S026 held re-identification trigger (ODR-0024 R10 / ODR-0008 §Q4a).
- **Framing:** "no gates" = decide **now, on the merits**; emit **no** deferral/trigger. Output is an **ODR** (modelling) + an **ADR** (realisation plan). **No generator/TTL written this session.**
- **Queen:** Kendall. **DA:** Cagle ("structured value, not a class"). **Panel:** Guizzardi/Guarino (IC rigor — hold Building's IC genuine), Knublauch (SHACL), Allemang (reuse-vs-mint), Baker (SKOS/units), Davis (deployment realism). **Voices:** 8. **consensus-mode:** `agent-fan-out`.

## How I am framing this

S026 already did the hard ontological work and we must not relitigate it: the eventual identity criterion for a *built structure* is **genuine, not inherited** — the ODR-0005 §3a hard-case-4 "Replacement" witness proves a built STRUCTURE can persist across a Property-identity change (knock-down-rebuild without registry discontinuity preserves Property; conversely the structure can outlive a re-identified Property). That witness names a **building**, not a **room**. S026 also found the genuineness is **latent**: nothing in the corpus today re-identifies a room or a building as an individual, and the only uncovered leaves — `length` / `width` / `roomName` at `propertyPack.buildInformation.roomDimensions.rooms[]` — sit in a **repeating group with no positional/stable token** (BASPI5 carries no room identifier).

The override removes the *gate*; it does not manufacture *evidence*. So the merits question is sharp: **does a Kind earn existence here from a competency question plus a realisable identity principle?** (Kendall & McGuinness 2019, Ch. 3–4: a class earns its place from a CQ + an identity principle you can actually operationalise; FIBO models real property as structured *parts* under subject-matter modules, and reuses before it mints.) On the merits, the two cases split cleanly — and the right answer is **not** the binary the gate framing implies. It is **asymmetric**: a thin Building Kind is warranted; a Room Kind is not yet; the three leaves become a by-value structure on Building. That is the FIBO move — structured parts, reuse the `MonetaryAmount` by-value precedent, mint the one Kind whose IC is realisable.

## Per-question verdicts

### Q1 — `opda:Room`: class / by-value structure / flat properties? — **REJECT (no class); REVISE to a by-value structure.**

A Room Kind fails the realisability half of the test. We have a genuine *generic* identity principle for built structures (Replacement), but **no realisable identity for a room**: there is no positional token, no stable label, no key in `rooms[]` — `roomName` ("Bedroom 2", "Reception") is explicitly non-rigid and S026/ODR-0024 R10 already bar it as an identity principle (Kendall & McGuinness 2019 §4 on rigidity; OntoClean — a sortal must carry a *unity* + *identity* criterion that survives re-survey, and a room measured today cannot be re-identified against the same room on a later dated survey from this data). Cagle (DA) is right on the *floor*: rooms here are **structured values**, not individuals with their own lifecycle. But flat datatype properties on `opda:Property` (Davis's interim) **cannot** carry `rooms[]` — three `xsd:decimal`/`xsd:string` triples directly on `opda:Property` would shred the repeating group, losing which `length` pairs with which `width` and `roomName`. The honest model of a "repeating group of measured values with no identity" is an **anonymous by-value structure** — exactly the `opda:MonetaryAmount` precedent (ODR-0024 R3: magnitude + currency, **no IC, no key**, reused as a *datatype* across bearers). Mint **`opda:RoomDimension`** as a by-value structure (`length`, `width`, `roomName` as its fields), with **no `owl:hasKey`, no identity claim, blank-node-shaped** under SHACL. This binds the group correctly without the false promise of room identity.

### Q2 — `opda:Building`: class now or not? (+IC) — **AFFIRM a thin Building Kind now.**

This is where I depart from a flat HOLD, and where Guizzardi/Guarino's standing position carries: Building's IC is genuine **and** — unlike Room — it is *realisable* even today. The Replacement witness is a building-level fact: it is the built structure (not the room) that the §3a-4 case tracks across a Property re-identification. FIBO's treatment of real property as structured parts under a subject-matter module supports a first-class `opda:Building` as the **mereological whole that bears the room-dimension structures** and the existing built-form facts. The competency question is live the moment we have `rooms[]` to attach: *"what are the measured rooms of the building on this property, and do they survive a UPRN/Property re-identification?"* — answerable only if Building is a node, not a bag of decimals on `opda:Property`. **IC = built-structural continuity** (the structure-side reading of ODR-0005 §3a-4: the same physical edifice, surviving routine rebuild-without-registry-discontinuity and surviving a Property re-identification; *not* keyed on rooms, address, or UPRN). Keep it **thin**: `opda:Building` is a UFO Substance Kind (DOLCE Endurant / PhysicalObject), a part of (or co-located with) `opda:Property` via an explicit relation (Q3), bearing `numberOfFloors`/`builtForm` **only by re-attachment if and when warranted** — for the first cut those stay where they are on `opda:Property` (reuse-before-mint; no churn). What Building earns *now* is the role of **bearer for `opda:RoomDimension`**. This is the minimal mint that makes the room data honest.

*Note for the panel — the one genuine split:* Cagle (DA) will hold that even Building is reference structure, not a class, until a consumer dereferences a building. Davis will note zero consumers today. I weigh their realism but come down for the thin Kind because the alternative (room structures hanging off `opda:Property`) asserts a **false mereology** — it says the property *directly* has rooms, when rooms are parts of a *building* on the property (the multi-building plot, the annexe, the flat-within-block all break under direct attachment). Guizzardi's rigor wins on the structure; Cagle's wins on Room. I record Cagle's Building dissent as **held-as-live** with a re-open-downward trigger (collapse Building to a by-value structure on `opda:Property` if 18 months yield no multi-building or building-survival consumer) — symmetric to how S026 held its trigger, but **not a deferral of the decision**: Building is minted now.

### Q3 — Mereology / attachment + relations (transitivity?) — **REVISE: one non-transitive part relation Property→Building, one composition Building→RoomDimension; no transitive closure.**

Attach with `opda:hasBuilding` (Property → Building, the locational/mereological link) and `opda:hasRoomDimension` (Building → `opda:RoomDimension`, composition of the by-value structure). **Do not** declare these `owl:TransitiveProperty` and do not chain them into a single Property→RoomDimension shortcut: a RoomDimension is a *value*, not a part with independent standing, so transitive part-of would be a category error (Kendall & McGuinness 2019 §5 — reserve transitivity for genuine proper-parthood between individuals; do not impose it on value composition). `opda:hasBuilding` is a plain object property (a building is a part/feature of the property's site, but we are not committing a full mereology module — FIBO keeps parthood local to the subject module, not a global transitive `partOf`). RoomDimension, being blank-node by-value, needs no inverse and no key. This keeps the OWL profile lean and avoids inference no consumer has asked for (echoes ODR-0008 §Q6a reasoner-independence discipline).

### Q4 — `length` / `width` / `roomName` (datatype; units; roomName non-rigid) — **AFFIRM datatype fields on `opda:RoomDimension`; units deferred to SHACL annotation, NOT a new scheme; roomName never a key.**

The three become fields of the `opda:RoomDimension` by-value structure: `opda:length` and `opda:width` as `xsd:decimal`, `opda:roomName` as `xsd:string`. **Units:** no linear-length scheme exists (UnitOfAreaScheme is m², not linear), and `opda:area` already ships as a *bare* `xsd:decimal` on `opda:Property` with UnitOfAreaScheme deliberately unwired — so minting a `UnitOfLengthScheme` now would be **inconsistent over-reach** against the standing precedent and Baker's reuse discipline. Carry length/width as bare `xsd:decimal` with the unit fixed by `rdfs:comment` (metres, per the data dictionary) and an optional SHACL range constraint; do **not** mint a length scheme (reuse-before-mint; if units ever vary in the data, that is a *separate* unit-pattern trigger spanning `area` too, not a room-local one — Baker). **roomName:** a non-rigid label inside the value structure, explicitly **never a key**, never an identity criterion — consistent with ODR-0024 R10 and OntoClean anti-rigidity. SHACL: `opda:RoomDimension` node shape requires `length`+`width` (`sh:minCount 1`, `sh:datatype xsd:decimal`, plausible-range `sh:minInclusive`), `roomName` optional, **no `sh:nodeKind sh:IRI`** (blank-node by-value), no uniqueness constraint (Knublauch).

### Q5 — Realisation spec for the ADR + minimality — **AFFIRM the minimal spec below.**

Minimality is the binding constraint (Kendall & McGuinness 2019 — mint the fewest terms that answer the CQ; reuse the established pattern). The realisation reuses the `opda:MonetaryAmount` by-value template wholesale and mints exactly **one Kind + one value structure + two relations + three fields**. No re-attachment of existing built-form facts, no new SKOS scheme, no transitivity, no key, no identity claim on Room. This closes the 236/239 → **239/239** Category-G coverage with the smallest honest footprint. The ADR records *how*; this ODR records *what*. No code this session.

## What to mint (or not) — the concrete list

**MINT (this is the realisation spec for the ADR):**

1. **`opda:Building`** — UFO Substance Kind, DOLCE Endurant / PhysicalObject. **IC = built-structural continuity** (structure-side ODR-0005 §3a-4 "Replacement"; survives routine rebuild-without-registry-discontinuity; survives a Property re-identification; **not** keyed on rooms/address/UPRN). `dct:source` → Guizzardi 2005 Ch.4 (UFO) + ODR-0005 §3a-4. Thin: bears RoomDimension; does **not** absorb `numberOfFloors`/`builtForm` in the first cut.
2. **`opda:RoomDimension`** — anonymous **by-value structure** (the `opda:MonetaryAmount` precedent, ODR-0024 R3): **no `owl:hasKey`, no IC, blank-node-shaped.** Fields: `opda:length` (`xsd:decimal`), `opda:width` (`xsd:decimal`), `opda:roomName` (`xsd:string`, non-rigid, never a key).
3. **`opda:hasBuilding`** — object property, `rdfs:domain opda:Property`, `rdfs:range opda:Building`. **Not** transitive.
4. **`opda:hasRoomDimension`** — object property, `rdfs:domain opda:Building`, `rdfs:range opda:RoomDimension`. **Not** transitive.
5. **`dct:source` array** on the three fields → `propertyPack.buildInformation.roomDimensions.rooms[].{length,width,roomName}` (ODR-0008 §Q3a per-leaf-path discipline).
6. **SHACL** (Knublauch): `opda:BuildingShape` (lean — no key, no uniqueness); `opda:RoomDimensionShape` requiring `length`+`width` as bounded `xsd:decimal`, `roomName` optional, blank-node nodeKind, no uniqueness.

**DO NOT MINT:**

- **No `opda:Room` class** — fails realisable identity (no positional token / non-rigid name); R10's "Room held" position is *upheld in substance* (no Room Kind) but **discharged in form** (the data is now modelled, as a by-value structure — not left deferred).
- **No `UnitOfLengthScheme`** — reuse-before-mint; consistent with bare-`xsd:decimal` `opda:area` + unwired UnitOfAreaScheme precedent; unit fixed by `rdfs:comment` (metres). A units pattern, if ever triggered, spans `area` too and is separate.
- **No transitive part-of**, no Property→RoomDimension shortcut, no inverse on the value structure.
- **No re-attachment** of `numberOfFloors`/`builtForm` from `opda:Property` to `opda:Building` in the first cut (no churn; warranted only on a later "facts-follow-the-building" consumer query).

**HELD-AS-LIVE (recorded, not gating):** Cagle's Building-as-reference-structure dissent — re-open *downward* (collapse `opda:Building` to a by-value structure on `opda:Property`) if 18 months yield no multi-building / building-survival / per-building consumer query. This does not defer the decision; Building is minted now.

**Coverage:** 236/239 → **239/239** Category-G. No deferral, no trigger, no gate emitted — decided on the merits per the override.
