## Context + the pre-flight finding

The operator asked the council to adjudicate, after a four-stream sourced review, whether OPDA should retain UFO at all. The pre-flight grounding (verified against the corpus; Knublauch and Smith both re-checked the tree before voting):

- **OPDA does not import UFO.** It uses UFO three restrained ways: gUFO advisory `rdf:type gufo:Quality` markers on 5 descriptive leaves, **quarantined to the annotation graph** (ADR-0034; `opda-descriptive-annotations.ttl`, graph `…/graph/descriptive-annotations`, which *points at* the class graph from outside it); the seven-category framework as advisory `opda:ufoCategory` SKOS tags (ODR-0011 §8a); and OntoClean's rigidity/identity tests as the "OWL subclass vs coded SKOS facet" decision procedure (ODR-0027).
- **The reasoning is shallow and the gUFO types are inert.** Per ODR-0029 only `rdfs:subClassOf` type-propagation fires; domain/range are excluded and validated as ~273 SHACL shapes. `gufo:Quality` carries zero subclass axioms, so nothing propagates — deleting every tag changes zero SHACL reports, zero inferences, zero emitted bytes.
- **It is a UFO+DOLCE hybrid.** Of the **47** `opda:ufoCategory` tags, **35** read "Quale-in-Region" — a **DOLCE** construct (Masolo et al., WonderWeb D18, 2003), not strictly UFO.
- **The strongest sourced critique is the gUFO authors' own** (Almeida, Guizzardi, Sales & Fonseca, 2026): UFO's modal/mereological apparatus cannot be captured in decidable OWL 2 DL → push constraints to SHACL. This *validates* OPDA's architecture rather than indicting it.
- **Domain fit favours UFO for the social/legal core** (UFO-C social commitments/claims; UFO-L's Hohfeldian Claim-Right↔Duty); **BFO** wins only on ISO/IEC 21838-2 standardisation (and is itself entering ISO standardisation for UFO); **Bernabé et al. (2023)** found no measurable benefit from foundational ontologies in practice, complexity the universal cost; **no property/built-environment standard** uses a foundational ontology today.

## Proposition P

"OPDA should RETAIN UFO as a lightweight design lens — gUFO advisory annotations quarantined to the annotation graph, the seven-category framework as descriptive SKOS tags, and OntoClean as the subclass-vs-coded-facet decision procedure — shipping pragmatic SHACL/SKOS/JSON. Rather than (a) deepening to a heavyweight OntoUML/full-UFO import, (b) switching to BFO, or (c) dropping the foundational layer entirely."

## Questions + verdicts

- **Q1 — Retain UFO-as-lens, or drop for pragmatic SHACL+SKOS only?** **AFFIRM 6-0** (DA REJECT push WITHDRAWN). But the live cross-examination *scoped* the affirmation precisely: UFO is **not** "grounding the model." Its **Relator/Role/RoleMixin category** is load-bearing for the agent-and-authority spine (~a handful of classes); **OntoClean** (separable from UFO, predating it) carries the rigidity/identity calls; and **register-deference** governs the 35 quality-value codes, which should drop the UFO byline. AFFIRM, scoped to where UFO is *categorially* doing work.
- **Q2 — Affirm OPDA should NOT deepen to OntoUML/full-UFO import?** **AFFIRM 6-0, unanimous.** The gUFO authors' own undecidability result is decisive: deepening imports non-computable commitments OPDA would only route around — modelling tax, zero additional validated triple.
- **Q3 — Thin optional BFO/IAO alignment: adopt-now / hold-in-reserve / reject?** **LAYERED 6-0.** The Smith↔Guizzardi exchange improved on every opening position by splitting the question by *where the join-target lives*: **ADOPT-NOW** the IAO information-artefact crosswalk (internal join-target — OPDA's own document/record family; external, referenced-not-imported, on the `prov:Entity` precedent); **HOLD-IN-RESERVE** the BFO continuant/occurrent upper spine + the "ISO-21838-aligned" credential (spec now, instantiate only against a real conformance clause); the **deontic core stays UFO-L**, uncontested.
- **Q4 — Honesty dispositions (a/b/c)?** **AFFIRM 6-0.** (a) "UFO-informed", not "UFO-grounded with guarantees" — DA-and-proponent consensus; (b) name the lineage as "UFO's DOLCE-derived quality structure" via a machine-readable `skos:scopeNote` on `opda:ufoCategory`; (c) flag UFO-C as the least-mature layer.

## Dialectic

**The OntoClean ≠ UFO wedge — the session's intellectual core (Cagle vs Guizzardi).** Cagle conceded the strongest pro-proponent facts up front — the annotations are artefact-inert (ODR-0029 quarantine confirmed), and a meta-property judgement *did* move emitted bytes (he verified Guizzardi's three named cases in the corpus: `tenureKind` `+R∧−I` deleted a dangling `skos:exactMatch`-to-subclass binding; `VouchEvidence`'s `+D` relator test overruled a directing-authority "collapse to facet" directive; `RiskAssessment`'s `+I` test — *Cagle's own prior ODR-0008d held dissent* — retained a class). He then narrowed his entire Q1 dissent to one falsifiable claim: the rigidity/identity/dependence meta-properties are **Guarino & Welty's OntoClean** (2000–2002), which *predate and are separable from UFO* — so "retain OntoClean-as-plain-judgement, ship SHACL+SKOS, **retire the UFO vocabulary**." His test to Guizzardi: *show one modelling call that needs UFO's category taxonomy, not merely OntoClean's meta-properties.* **Guizzardi met it at the Relator:** OntoClean is a theory of meta-properties and is *silent by construction* on sortal categories — it cannot express "the Relator *founds* the RoleMixins" or "joint-tenancy-vs-tenants-in-common is a property of the Relator, not the Roles" (Guizzardi 2005 Ch. 4 §4.4). `opda:Proprietorship`/`opda:Transaction` ship as Relators that found `Seller`/`Buyer` (ODR-0006/0007). **"OntoClean necessary, not sufficient; the sufficiency is UFO's."** The convergence: UFO is load-bearing *only at the Relator/Role spine* — exactly the relational-endurant layer where UFO-L's Hohfeldian apparatus also lives — inert everywhere else, reasoned-over nowhere. Both sides conceded against interest: Guizzardi conceded the 35 "Quale-in-Region" tags are **register-deference** (EPC bands, HMLR tenure codes, INSPIRE classes are coded `sh:in` because an external authority closes the value space, *not* by a rigidity judgement) and "should stop being presented as UFO doing work"; Cagle conceded he could show the layer is *optional*, not *harmful*, and "REJECT requires net harm."

**The final convergence — co-signed (the council's central deliverable).** Pressed for the single sharpest instance, Guizzardi named **`opda:numberOfSellers` and its *placement* on `opda:Proprietorship`** (verified at `opda-agent.ttl:155` — "the cardinality of the owner-set the Proprietorship Relator mediates"; the governing rule "joint-tenancy vs tenants-in-common is a property of the Relator, NOT of the Roles"). OntoClean's `+R/+I/+D` are **monadic** meta-properties of a single type, but placement is a **relational/topology** question — on which node of a mediated structure the aggregation fact lives — and **OntoClean has no mediation primitive**, so no meta-property value on any type outputs "put it on the Relator, not the borne Roles." Cagle verified the diff in the corpus (`opda-classes.ttl:43`, `opda-agent.ttl:155`, `opda-transaction.ttl:36`), **conceded the Relator wedge in full**, and withdrew option (c) as stated → a *scoped* AFFIRM. He then handed a sharpening Guizzardi adopted: what is strictly irreducible is the **relational-reification primitive** (reify the relation as a first-class node; that node owns the relation's aggregation/modal attributes; relata bind as borne roles) — and **UFO's Relator is the apt constructor OPDA *chose* to express it, not the unique source.** The corpus records this as a choice-with-co-precedent: `opda:Transaction` cites *both* "UFO Relator (Guizzardi 2005 Ch. 4 §4.4)" *and* "FIBO Arrangement precedent" (`opda-transaction.ttl:36,39`); DnS qua-individuals and a plain reified n-ary relation supply the same shape. **The bounded reason UFO specifically earns the choice is the deontic core:** among the reification-primitive suppliers, only UFO+UFO-L natively unifies reification *and* Hohfeldian Claim-Right↔Duty correlativity, which a property transaction (Hohfeld throughout — charges, covenants, easements, offer/acceptance) needs; FIBO gives the shape but not the correlativity, DnS is deontically neutral, BFO must reify a claim-right as a directive-information-entity. **Guizzardi's Relator axis (the Cagle exchange) and Smith's UFO-L deontic axis (Q3) are literally the same ~handful-of-classes layer** — that convergence *is* the retention case, precisely scoped. The co-signed joint finding (Cagle + Guizzardi, verbatim): *"UFO is not 'grounding' the model; UFO's Relator/Role category is **load-bearing** for the agent-and-authority spine — a handful of classes — OntoClean earns the rigidity/identity calls without the foundation, and register-deference (not either) governs the quality codes, which should stop wearing a UFO byline. **Necessary-and-bounded, not pervasive-and-grounding.**"*

**The honest verb (Q4a — DA-proponent consensus).** The two adversaries agreed the wire format reasons with nothing, so the honest claim is "UFO-**informed**", never "UFO-**grounded** with guarantees." Guizzardi's settled formulation: *"OPDA's modelling process is governed by a UFO/OntoClean decision procedure; the artefact carries an inert provenance record of that governance."* Allemang's frame: *"keep the lens that sharpened the knife, ship only the knife."* Knublauch anchored the precedent — ODR-0029 already renamed "OWL 2 RL Safe Group" → "RDFS-Plus" to kill an over-claim; the same honesty obligation applies to the foundational layer's label.

**The Q3 layered settlement (Smith ↔ Guizzardi, joint, corpus-verified).** Smith pressed ADOPT-NOW well but conceded the deontic core to UFO-L *unprompted*: *"UFO-L natively formalises Hohfeldian legal positions … BFO has no native legal apparatus; it must reify a Claim-Right as a directive information entity. I hold BFO in reserve for the upper spine, I do NOT claim it models your deontic core more naturally than UFO-L. It does not."* Guizzardi, moved partially, split the question by join-target: the **IAO information-artefact crosswalk** has an *internal* join-target (OPDA's own document/record/EPC/`AttachedDocument` family ≈ `obo:IAO`), verifiable today, on the mechanism OPDA already runs (`opda:EPCCertificate rdfs:subClassOf prov:Entity` — referenced, never imported) → **ADOPT-NOW**; the **BFO upper spine + credential** has an *external* join-target (other TLO-aligned ontologies — an empty set in the built environment today, per Bernabé) and would re-import the exact assert-more-than-you-perform defect S035/S036 purged → **HOLD, specified-and-ready**. Smith sharpened the adoption trigger to make it testable: the credential is earned not when edges exist but when an ISO-21838 conformance clause is on an *actual* contract the bridge satisfies. *Same crosswalk file, two maturity triggers.*

**The quarantine is load-bearing (Knublauch's standing condition).** Knublauch's AFFIRM-Q1 is explicitly conditional: *"UFO-as-lens is retained on the standing condition that gUFO annotations remain quarantined to the annotation graph, outside the SHACL shapes and outside the reasoned union — the moment that's breached, the retention lapses."* He verified the three CI-enforced guards (three-graph separation ODR-0004 §3a; annotation-graph home ODR-0010 §Q7a; the ODR-0029 regime that excludes domain/range and cannot propagate the advisory types). Strip any one and his vote flips to REJECT.

**The disclosure condition (Guarino's swing on Q4b).** Guarino — the OntoClean/DOLCE authority — concurred that the quality structure is UFO *inheriting* DOLCE's Quality/Quale/Region (D18), not a foreign graft, and endorsed Allemang's "UFO's DOLCE-derived quality structure" over "bolted-together hybrid." But his Q4(b) AFFIRM carries one non-negotiable condition that *is* his swing vote: the dominant-construct fact must be **legible at the artefact level, not buried in an ADR** — a machine-readable `skos:scopeNote` on `opda:ufoCategory` stating (i) the categories are UFO's, (ii) UFO's quality categories descend from DOLCE's Quality/Quale/Region, (iii) the majority of OPDA's tagged properties fall under that quality apparatus. *"A foundational scheme that hides its own centre of gravity has abandoned the one service it exists to render."*

**The DA, and why he withdrew (Cagle).** Cagle's red line was net harm, and he could not demonstrate it: the runtime fork-cost is ~zero (quarantine — conceded to Smith, whose "no metaphysics reaches the reasoner → no forked foundation to strip" answered the fork-cost thesis), and the analytic judgement demonstrably did work (conceded to Guizzardi). What he *held*: the governance/maintenance fork-cost — the UFO+DOLCE mapping and 47 tags must be maintained and defended as the model grows — and the strict-separability of OntoClean from UFO (the foundation *vocabulary* is droppable, hence *optional*). His disposition: **Q1 WITHDRAWN** (REJECT push withdrawn → default AFFIRM-the-proposition), **held-as-live** with three precise re-open triggers; **Q3 CONCEDED HOLD** (REJECT-the-reserve withdrawn, conditional on a named adoption trigger); **Q2/Q4 AFFIRM** with the majority. His extracted finding, which the council adopts: *"UFO-informed provenance over a SHACL+SKOS+OntoClean spine, not UFO-grounded anything — that distinction is the thing I came to extract."*

## Dispositions

All `status: proposed` — the operator ratifies adoption. Routed to **ODR-0030**.

1. **Retain UFO as a design-time lens, scoped to the Relator/Role/RoleMixin spine (Q1).** gUFO `rdf:type` + `ufoCategory` tags stay **annotation-graph-only** (ODR-0029 quarantine + ODR-0010 §Q7a + ODR-0004 §3a are load-bearing — retention lapses if breached, per Knublauch's standing condition). Publish the honest verb: *the modelling process is governed by a UFO/OntoClean decision procedure; the artefact carries an inert provenance record of that governance.* Kill any prose implying the wire format reasons with UFO.
2. **Relabel the 35 quality-value tags as register-deference (Q1 / Q4b).** The "Quale-in-Region" tags whose value space is closed by an external authority (EPC, HMLR, INSPIRE) are coded `sh:in` by register-deference, not by a UFO/OntoClean judgement — the docs/`scopeNote` must stop presenting them as UFO doing work.
3. **Record the three Q1 re-open triggers as live conditions** (Cagle's held dissent): (i) the UFO/`ufoCategory` layer is ever made reasoned-over (moved out of the annotation-graph quarantine into a graph that drives inference or SHACL); (ii) the 35/47 DOLCE-under-a-UFO-label mismatch is shown to have caused a downstream modelling error or maintainer miscategorisation; (iii) maintaining the foundation mapping measurably delays or blocks a council decision.
4. **No deepening to OntoUML/full-UFO import (Q2).** The gUFO authors' OWL-2-DL undecidability finding is the technical warrant.
5. **ADOPT-NOW: the IAO information-artefact crosswalk (Q3).** A thin, external, **referenced-not-imported** set of `skos:exactMatch`/`closeMatch`/`rdfs:subClassOf` edges from OPDA's document/record family (title registers, EPCs, searches, requisitions, the `AttachedDocument` line) to `obo:IAO` (document / information-content-entity / directive), in the annotation graph, on the `prov:Entity` precedent — never `owl:imports`, never reasoned over.
6. **HOLD-IN-RESERVE: the BFO continuant/occurrent spine + "ISO-21838-aligned" credential (Q3).** Write the crosswalk spec now; instantiate the edges and bank the credential **only** when an ISO-IEC 21838 conformance clause exists on an actual contract/tender the bridge satisfies. Interim honest claim is the narrow true one ("OPDA carries deliberate IAO crosswalks"), never the broad inert one ("ISO-21838-aligned").
7. **Deontic core remains UFO-L (Q3).** Hohfeldian Claim-Right↔Duty (charges, covenants, easements, requisitions) stays UFO-L-shaped; the bridge never touches it. Uncontested by both Guizzardi and Smith — the strongest domain-fit statement in the council.
8. **Honesty dispositions (Q4).** (a) publish "UFO-informed", not "UFO-grounded with guarantees"; (b) add a `skos:scopeNote` on `opda:ufoCategory` naming "UFO's DOLCE-derived quality structure" per Guarino's 3-part condition; (c) mark UFO-C (social/intentional/legal) as the least-mature layer wherever the standard leans on it.
9. **Do NOT:** move the gUFO/`ufoCategory` annotations into a reasoned graph; import OntoUML or BFO; bank an "ISO-aligned" credential off inert edges; present the 35 quality-value tags as UFO doing categorial work.

**Held-as-live dissent (Cagle):** *OntoClean is strictly separable from UFO, so the foundation **vocabulary** is droppable — the layer is optional, not harmful.* WITHDRAWN as a REJECT push (no net harm shown at ~zero runtime cost), recorded as held-as-live. **Re-open triggers:** the three Q1 conditions above; if any fires, REJECT (retire the UFO vocabulary, keep OntoClean-as-judgement + SHACL + SKOS) is revisited. Recorded in ODR-0030 §Held dissent + §Alternatives.
