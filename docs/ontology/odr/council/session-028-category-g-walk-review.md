## Context

The curated Category-G descriptive walk (ADR-0031 work-items 1–2) landed: 156 candidate-G leaves minted as flat datatype/object properties, 29 collapsed (free-text→`opda:disclosureDetail`, monetary→`opda:price`, `uprn`/`address`→existing predicates), 3 bearer classes minted (`opda:NearbyFacility`/`School`/`HealthCareFacility`), 3 leaves deferred (`opda:Room`). All six CI gates green; `ci-category-g-coverage` reported 185/188. This session puts the modelling decisions to a panel for ratification before the walk is treated as settled. The mappings + nine framed questions were the agenda.

**Two briefing corrections the panel surfaced (recorded for the record):**
1. The `opda:Room` held-as-live decision is **ODR-0008 §Q4a** (conditional class promotion), not ADR-0011 (which is module-TBox emission). The agenda mis-cited it.
2. The facility classes were minted citing **ODR-0008 §Q4a** as warrant — but §Q4a is a *provenance / lifecycle / PII* test whose named promotions are all Information Objects (Survey/EPC/Search/Valuation/Comparable) + the held Building/Room. A nearby-facility POI meets none of §Q4a's three criteria; the cite does not license the classes (Hendler BLOCKER, Allemang, Guizzardi-lineage-note). The classes' *warrant* — not necessarily the classes — is the defect.

## Decision — verdicts and dispositions

### Q1 — `opda:NearbyFacility` / `School` / `HealthCareFacility` promotion → **REVISE**
Split panel. **Guizzardi (UFO):** the Substance-Kind meta-category is *correct* — a school/hospital is a mind-independent endurant with its own identity, "NOT a Quality of opda:Property — the same facility neighbours many properties"; the genus is a proper sortal Kind-genus, School/HealthCareFacility proper Subkinds with inherited IC; AFFIRM (+ mint `opda:TransportNode` for the third band, for symmetry). **Allemang (Queen):** the §Q4a cite doesn't license them and no consumer query forces the genus/subkind split — collapse to ONE flat `opda:NearbyFacility` bearer, drop the subkinds until a query forces it. **Hendler (DA):** BLOCKER — minted under a borrowed §Q4a cite that does not hold; the textbook speculative-hierarchy failure.

**Queen resolution:** The §Q4a citation is wrong by 3-way agreement and **must be removed** — that clears the formal defect. On structure, the panel genuinely splits between Guizzardi's precise-bearer subkinds and Allemang's flat-genus simplicity. For the first cut: **keep `opda:NearbyFacility` (all three seats accept it as a sound bearer), re-warrant it as a UFO Substance-Kind promotion (Guizzardi's S028 ruling: a referenced facility is an endurant with its own IC, not a Property Quality) — NOT §Q4a; collapse `School`/`HealthCareFacility` into the genus for now** (school/healthcare props become flat properties on `NearbyFacility`, SHACL-scoped per band in the form profiles), with the Subkind split + `TransportNode` recorded as **Guizzardi's held dissent** (re-open trigger: a consumer query that needs per-band typing, or the ODR-0023 R2 axis review). **Also REVISE the `schoolType` bands** (`opda:primary`/`secondary`/`private`/`college`/`nursery`): all seats object — they are range-less object properties pointing at no emitted node, and bare generic IRIs (`opda:private`, `opda:primary`) are permanent-namespace landmines (Hendler OBJ-6a, Allemang "enum in an object-property costume"). Replace with a single `opda:schoolType` datatype property over a SKOS `opda:SchoolTypeScheme`.

### Q2 — `opda:Room` activation → **AFFIRM (keep held)**
Consensus. Guizzardi: a Room is a functional Building-part with relational/extrinsic identity; `roomName` is a non-rigid label, not an identity principle — minting `opda:Room` now multiplies a Kind without an IC. Allemang: the re-open trigger ("first named BASPI5 round-trip query exercising sub-Property reasoning") has not fired; emit nothing speculative. Verified `opda:Room` is not emitted anywhere. **`length`/`width`/`roomName` stay deferred candidate-G.** When activated: a Building-part bearing measured Qualities, promoted to a part-Kind only on a named query.

### Q3 — monetary leaves → **REVISE (remove the false collapse; defer to the monetary walk)**
**The decisive finding.** Hendler + Kendall read the curation register (`category_g_curation.py`): **18 economically-distinct G monetary leaves** (`annualGroundRent`, `annualServiceCharge`, `holdingDeposit`, `securityDeposit`, `rent`, `soldPrice`, `listPrice`, `estimatedPrice`, fees, deed/permit costs, …) are collapsed onto `opda:price`. Guizzardi + Allemang read the emitted TTL, where `opda:price` is scoped to **fixtures-and-fittings only** (ODR-0022 §4, transaction-scoped). **Both readings are correct, and together they expose the defect:** the register reuses the Category-D *fixtures* price for Category-G *headline* amounts, which ODR-0022 §1/G1 explicitly keeps distinct, and which conflates a sale price, a recurring ground rent, a refundable deposit, and a service charge under one bare `xsd:decimal` (Hendler "formal incoherence"; Kendall "destroys distinctions a lender's query needs"; Guizzardi "these inhere in different bearers — Property / LegalEstate-Relator / Transaction — several are Relator facets, not Qualities").

**Queen resolution:** **Remove the 18 monetary leaves from the COLLAPSED→`price` register; re-mark them DEFERRED to the held Category-G monetary-leaf walk** (ODR-0008d item-3). At that walk, per the panel's standing requirement: a value-structured **`opda:MonetaryAmount`** (magnitude + currency, ISO-4217 SKOS, currency-defaulting-GBP via profile — Kendall/Guizzardi-REQUIRED), reused as the **datatype** across distinct per-economic-kind properties (`askingPrice`/`groundRent`/`serviceCharge`/`deposit`/`fee`…), each bound to its **own bearer** ("reuse the datatype, never the bearer" — Guizzardi). The fixtures `opda:price` itself stands as a sanctioned single-currency interim (Allemang) — but **fix its comment**, which over-claims a "MonetaryAmount pattern" that does not exist in the corpus (Kendall + Hendler). `potentialCost` is a monetary leaf mis-filed under free-text (Allemang Q4) → moves here. **Honest coverage drops 185 → ~167/188** (the 18 monetary + 3 Room are genuinely not-yet-done; collapsing the 18 onto the fixtures price was false coverage).

### Q4 — free-text → `opda:disclosureDetail` → **AFFIRM** (minus `potentialCost`)
Consensus (Cagle, Allemang). All 9 are `rdfs:comment`-grade prose with no identity criterion or structured payload to discard; the question is carried by subject + instance `dct:source` (ODR-0022 §Rules.1/.6). Cagle confirmed the regulator-salient remedial prose (building-safety/spray-foam/knotweed `details`) is NOT swallowed — the §3 salience carve-out routes those to G/residue first. **Re-route `potentialCost`** to the monetary disposition (Q3); the other 8 stand.

### Q5 — domain-less `name`/`displayName`/`mediaUrl`/`url` → **AFFIRM** (no superclass)
Unanimous. A polysemous label (`name` ×46) has no single rigid bearer; an `rdfs:domain` superclass would license a reasoner to infer e.g. a Person *is* an Artefact — false. Domain-less datatype properties assert nothing unwarranted; SHACL property-shapes bind the local bearer per form. **SHOULD-FIX (Hendler OBJ-4):** confirm the profiles carry `sh:datatype xsd:anyURI` + a URI `sh:pattern` for `mediaUrl`/`url` so they are constrained somewhere.

### Q6 — `titleNumber` domain → **REVISE → `opda:RegisteredTitle`** (Allemang dissent recorded)
Split. **Kendall (FIBO/HMLR):** a title number identifies the *register record*, and `opda:RegisteredTitle`'s own IC is "title-number lineage + registry-event history"; the estate has no title number — the record does. Correct the domain; reach the estate via the existing `opda:recordsEstate` join. **Allemang:** the TTL deliberately models it as the estate's *cited* identifier (distinct from the record), already shipped. **Queen resolution:** Kendall's HMLR/identity argument is the more standard and is authoritative on this point — **REVISE `rdfs:domain` to `opda:RegisteredTitle`**; the third `dct:source` path (`…registerExtract.ocSummaryData.title.titleNumber`) is unambiguously the record. Low-stakes; Allemang's estate-side reading recorded as dissent (if the estate-side convenience is wanted, add a separate co-reference predicate, do not overload the identifier's domain).

### Q7 — doc-metadata → `opda:Document` (= `opda:DocumentEvidence`) → **REVISE**
Split 2–2. **Kendall + Guizzardi:** reuse is right — a registry-attached document *is* a document-evidence artefact / one Information-Object Kind under two roles; minting a parallel class over-multiplies. **Allemang + Hendler:** `opda:Document` is `owl:equivalentClass opda:DocumentEvidence`, so **every** instance bearing the four metadata props is *entailed* to be evidence carrying eIDAS-Substantial assurance — an over-commitment for plain `additionalDocuments[]` filing metadata. **Queen resolution:** the formal entailment is real (equivalentClass ⇒ all attached docs are evidence), and even Guizzardi's "one Kind, two roles" framing requires the roles to be *distinct classes*, not an equivalence. **REVISE:** break the conflation — introduce a neutral `opda:AttachedDocument` (with `opda:DocumentEvidence` as its evidence-playing subclass), re-home the four metadata props onto it; reserve the evidence class for artefacts actually standing as evidence under ODR-0009. Apply Guizzardi's caveat: give the document Kind an explicit IC (content + issuing activity, not `documentTypeCode`/`documentDate`).

### Q8 — G11 reconciliation (6 overlap leaves) → **RATIFY**
Unanimous (Allemang, Kendall). The six (`builtForm`/`centralHeatingFuelType`/`currentEnergyRating`/`heatingType`/`ownershipType`/`propertyType`) are Quale-in-Region of Property/LegalEstate with SKOS-scheme ranges, flat per §Q6a, `dct:source` tightened to G2 schema-leaf-paths; `ownershipType` correctly on `opda:LegalEstate`; `propertyType` C+G spanning per §Q3a; `currentEnergyRating` DESNZ-flagged per §3. The model the rest of the walk should imitate.

### Q-ID — bank-account leaves flat on `opda:Organisation` → **AFFIRM** (named promotion trigger)
Kendall. `accountName`/`accountNumber`/`sortCode` as flat `xsd:string` props (leading-zero-significant — correct) answer the only competency question present ("where to remit"). **Mint `opda:BankAccount` (⊑ a FIBO FinancialAccount) only on a named trigger:** an account shared across organisations, account-level lifecycle/validation (IBAN/BIC/mandate), or a query retrieving accounts independent of holder.

### Q9 — session-025 binning under-count → **REVISE the categoriser + re-run (report/scope only)**
Consensus (Cagle, Allemang, Hendler). The candidate-G set under-counts true Quale-in-Region G: the `if has_enum: return "C"` fallback in `leaf_categoriser.py` routes genuine enum-bearing Property attributes (`propertyType` split-defect, `ownerType`, `constructionType`, `priceQualifier`, `transportType`, `typeOfConnection`, `ofstedRating`, + substantive yesNo facts `hasBeenFlooded`/`isInsured`/…) to Category C; the S025 fix only added a 7-name allow-list. Allemang counts ~38 affected; Cagle names the set. **Decisive mitigant (all seats + the `opda-binning-report-vs-emitted` finding):** this is a **report/curation-scope miscount, not an emitted-artefact defect** — the affected terms that *are* emitted (`propertyType`, `hasBeenFlooded`, `isInsured`, …) are already correct G-grade in the TTL (flat datatype on Property/LegalEstate, `sh:in` a scheme). **Action:** replace the literal allow-list with a structural C-vs-G rule (status-flag-reused-across-contexts → C; substantive Property/estate Quality or Substance-Kind-label whose value-space is enumerated → G with the enum as its SKOS range), re-run `categorise-leaves` so candidate-G is honest (~200+). **No byte-identity re-pin** of already-curated terms. Track + add a regression test (Hendler: assert no new enum-leaf under a Property/estate path lands in C unexamined).

### Q-SKOS — datatype-vs-SKOS default → **AFFIRM default; REVISE execution** (mint schemes from live enums)
Cagle. The §Q5a "plain datatype unless the data carries an enum" default is sound; the five flagged free-string leaves (`documentTypeCode`, `pricingMethodology`, `typeOfHealthCare`, `religiousCharacter`, `supplyClassification`) correctly stay bare (no in-data enum). The miss is the inverse of Q9 — leaves carrying a **live enum** diverted to C without a SKOS range. **Mint ~5 schemes** from data-attested enums: `opda:ConstructionTypeScheme`, `opda:PriceQualifierScheme`, `opda:TransportTypeScheme` (on the transport band), `opda:BroadbandConnectionTypeScheme`, `opda:OfstedRatingScheme` (Ofsted-authority `dct:source`); **re-range** `ownerType`→`opda:OwnerTypeScheme`, `marketingTenure`→ the existing tenure scheme (reuse-before-mint, do not mint a third). Driven by enums the data actually carries — data-faithful, not vocabulary inflation.
